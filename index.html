Morosini Fountain: The Morosini fountain, or Lion fountain, is in the main square of Heraklion. It was built during the Venetian rule of Crete. Open Map

Rocca a Mare Fortress: Koules or Rocca a Mare is a massive two-story fortress that surrounds the harbor, and it is one of the most emblematic landmarks of Heraklion. Open Map

As a tip, we encourage you to walk the hidden path behind the fortress in the evening, as the sun sets. It has amazing views of the old port and it is a very relaxing activity. Afterwards, you can return to the center for dinner or drinks!
